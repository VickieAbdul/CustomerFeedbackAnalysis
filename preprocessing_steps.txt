Basic Steps to take when preprocessing texts:


* Load spaCy Model: Ensure you load the en_core_web_sm model for processing English text.
* Remove Stopwords & Punctuation: Filter out unnecessary words and symbols.
* Lemmatization: Convert words to their base forms (e.g., "running" ➔ "run").
* Lowercasing: Ensure consistency in the text.
